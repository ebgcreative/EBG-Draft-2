About EBG Creative

The person behind the brand.

The blueberry who represents her online.

EBG mascot image

Blueberry is what my mom has called me my whole life. It stuck. So when I built EBG Creative and needed a face for the brand, it could only ever be a blueberry. Meet EBG, the creative and professional side of me that shows up in everything we do online.

I started EBG Creative because I've been that person who figures it out as they go and realized that's actually a superpower.

In my final year of university I took on a marketing internship with a startup that had never had anyone in that role before. No playbook, no budget, no blueprint. Just me, a lot of curiosity, and the kind of eagerness that either burns you out or builds you up. For me it built me up. I watched strategies flop and learned more from those than anything else. By the time I graduated I knew this was exactly what I wanted to do.

I grew up in a small town where you knew the person behind the business and rooted for them. I have a lot of respect for people who take the risk to build something. That's how I show up for every client. Personally, genuinely, and always in their corner.
